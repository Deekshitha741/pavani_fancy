About Sri Pavani

Sri Pavani Jewellery was born from a deep love for traditional Indian craftsmanship. We bring you the finest 1 gram gold jewellery — pieces that carry the weight of tradition while being light enough for everyday elegance.

Every piece in our collection is thoughtfully designed to celebrate the beauty of Indian women — from the young bride adorning herself for the first time, to the grandmother who treasures her gold as a legacy.
